Jellyfin

Your personal Netflix,
without the monthly bill.

Jellyfin is your own private streaming service. Every movie and TV show in the library is hand-picked. Browse by genre, search for anything, and jump right in. It remembers what you've watched and picks up exactly where you left off — even if you switch from your phone to your TV.

See how to get started →
Immich

Your photos & videos,
beautifully preserved.

Immich automatically backs up every photo and video from your phone the moment you connect to Wi-Fi. Browse your memories in a gorgeous timeline, search by face or location, and create shared albums with family — all in complete privacy. Think of it as Google Photos, but yours alone.
