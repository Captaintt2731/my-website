What is Jellyfin?

How to watch — step by step

Step 1

Open Jellyfin in your browser

Go to jellyfin.teancumshomelab.com in any web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge — they all work). You can also bookmark it for easy access later.

💡 Tip: On your phone? Open this link in your phone's browser. You can also "Add to Home Screen" so it works like an app.
Step 2

Sign in with your credentials

Enter the username and password that Teancum gave you. If you've forgotten them, email teancum.stephens@gmail.com and he'll reset it for you. Check the "Remember me" box so you don't have to type it every time.

💡 Tip: Your login is just for you. Don't share it — if a family member wants access, ask Teancum to set up a separate account.
Step 3

Browse the library

After logging in you'll see the home screen with recently added content and suggestions. Use the top menu to browse Movies or TV Shows. You can also use the search bar (magnifying glass icon) to find something specific.

Step 4

Pick something and press play

Click any movie or show thumbnail to open it. You'll see a description, cast, and rating. Hit the big Play button to start watching. For TV shows, choose a season and episode first.

💡 Tip: If the video looks blurry or buffers, click the settings gear icon during playback and lower the quality. 1080p is usually the sweet spot.
Step 5

Add subtitles if needed

During playback, click the speech bubble icon (or press S) to choose subtitles. You can pick English or other languages. Subtitles can also be turned off if you don't need them.

Many smart TVs have a browser built in — search for Jellyfin from there. Alternatively, for the best experience:

Start playing a video in your browser on your computer or phone. Click the cast icon (looks like a rectangle with Wi-Fi waves) in the Jellyfin player to send it to your TV.

Frequently asked questions

The video keeps stopping or buffering — what do I do?

This usually means your internet connection is slower than needed for high-quality video. During playback, click the gear icon and select a lower quality (try 720p or 1080p instead of 4K). This usually fixes it instantly.

I forgot my password. How do I get back in?

Email teancum.stephens@gmail.com and Teancum will reset it for you quickly.

Can I download movies to watch offline?

Yes, but only on mobile apps. In the Jellyfin Android app or iOS via compatible players, there's a download option. In the browser version, downloads aren't available.

Can multiple people watch at the same time?

Yes! Multiple users can watch simultaneously. Each person should have their own account so their watch history and progress stays separate.

I don't see a movie I want. Can it be added?

Possibly! Email teancum.stephens@gmail.com with a request and Teancum will look into adding it.

Is this legal?

Jellyfin is free open-source software Teancum uses to organize and stream media he owns. This is a private library for approved guests only.
