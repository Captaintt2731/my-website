What is Immich?

Immich is like Google Photos or iCloud — but completely private and stored on Teancum's home server. No company can see your photos. No storage limits (within reason). No monthly fees.

Once you set up the mobile app, your photos and videos back up automatically whenever you're on Wi-Fi. You can then view, search, and share them from any browser or the app.

Getting started — step by step

Step 1

Sign in on the web

Open immich.teancumshomelab.com in any browser. Enter your username and password (provided by Teancum — email teancum.stephens@gmail.com to request one). You'll land on your photo timeline.

Step 2

Install the mobile app for auto-backup

This is the most important step — it's what keeps your photos safe automatically.

📱 iPhone / iPad
  1. Open the App Store
  2. Search for "Immich"
  3. Download and open it
  4. Enter the server address below
  5. Sign in with your username and password
🤖 Android
  1. Open the Google Play Store
  2. Search for "Immich"
  3. Download and open it
  4. Enter the server address below
  5. Sign in with your username and password

Server address to enter in the app

https://immich.teancumshomelab.com
💡 Tip: After logging in, go to the app settings and enable Background Backup. Your photos will now back up silently whenever you're on Wi-Fi — you don't have to do anything else.
Step 3

Browse your photo timeline

Once your photos have backed up, you'll see a beautiful timeline view — photos sorted by the most recent first. Scroll down to go further back in time. Tap or click any photo to view it full screen.

Step 4

Create an album

Albums are a great way to organize memories — a holiday, a birthday, a year. Click Albums in the left menu, then + Create Album. Give it a name, then browse your timeline to add photos to it.

💡 Tip: You can share an album with another Immich user — perfect for sharing holiday photos with family.
Step 5

Search for anything

Click the magnifying glass icon and type anything — "beach", "birthday cake", "dogs", or a person's name. Immich uses AI to understand what's in your photos without you having to tag them manually.

Frequently asked questions

Will this replace my iCloud / Google Photos backup?

It can work alongside it, or replace it — your choice. Immich backs up a separate copy. If you want to turn off iCloud/Google Photos to save money, just make sure Immich is running well first.

How much storage do I have?

Storage is managed by Teancum on the server. If you're getting close to a limit he'll let you know. In general, don't worry about it — just back up normally.

Can I delete photos from my phone after backup?

Yes, once photos are backed up to Immich you can safely delete them from your phone. They'll remain on the server. Just double-check the backup completed first.

Can my family see my photos?

No — your photos are private to your account by default. You choose what to share and with whom, through shared albums.

Does backup use my mobile data?

By default, Immich only backs up when you're on Wi-Fi. You can turn on cellular backup in the app settings if you want faster uploads, but it's off by default to save your data.

I lost my phone — are my photos safe?

Yes! As long as the photos were backed up before you lost the phone, they're safely stored on Teancum's server. Just log in from a new device.

Something isn't working — who do I contact?

Email teancum.stephens@gmail.com and Teancum will get back to you as soon as possible.
